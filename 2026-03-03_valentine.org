Even with international conficts bringing discord to families and communities alike, we shall celebrate the monumental history of Women's Day with all of our heart.

On the occasion of International Women's Day, Greenwich IT Club wish to send the best wishes to every kind soul out there, we should allow us and our partner the chance to cherish the beautiful feminine around us.
