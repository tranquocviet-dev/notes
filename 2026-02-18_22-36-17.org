* understanding the process of drawing (flow art)
** drawing should be a flow
- flow essentially means one stage is the foundation of the next
- the art should not be bound to one specific idea rather changing to fit the previous changes made
- the art should also reflect the changes of the artist in each iteration
- that said the piece afterwards should also understand that it must be each own thing instead of following strictly
** drawing should not involve "science"
- too many times i see people revolve their art around theories and logic, making their art not to much different one from another
- this makes the idea of "ever evolving" useless
- this implies that there is a "wrong" way to draw which is false
** beauty is only relevant in the eye of the preceiver
- of course opinion has to be recognized, but it should not dictate what the artist deem beautiful
- the real currency when comparing art is how much you like your new piece compared to your old one
