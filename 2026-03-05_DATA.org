* Data Visualization
** The 5 most important parts of data visualization
- Source
- Visualization tools
- Audience
- Storytelling
- Presentation
** Interactive Visualization
- Allows users to control various aspects of the display
- Response dynamically to user actions
- Filter and select specific parts of the data to focus on
- Adjust parameters or settings
*** Importance
- Enchancing User Engagement
- Facilitating Exploration
- Improved data relationship understanding
- Real time feedback and analysis
** Steps to Visualize data
- Capture the attention of audience
- Develop narrative
- Highlight insight
- Call to action
